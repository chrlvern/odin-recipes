Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread is a very sweet bread made with bananas.

It goes great with cream cheese and a cup of tea!

Ingredients

  • Ripe Bananas
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Eggs
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix mashed bananas and melted butter
  2. Add eggs, yogurt, and vanilla. Whish and set aside.
  3. Mix together flour, baking soda, salt and sugar in a seperate bowl.
  4. Fold wet and dry ingredients together
  5. Place mixture in a greased loaf pan and bake at 350F for 45 minutes.
